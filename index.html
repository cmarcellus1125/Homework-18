Chris Marcellus

UI/UX Designer

I come from a theatre family, a background of telling stories, so that is what I try to convey in my designs. I initially started with stagecraft and found that my skill could easily transfer over to UI/UX as it is the same thought process; giving users the best experience. From there, I easily pick up and learn programs such as Figma, Miro, Adobe XD; a pattern dating back to my youth. With these tools acquired, I have created what you see in this portfolio.

Work

inSpectrum

inSpectrum is a mobile app that measures UV data based on your location along with water consumption tracker, sunscreen application timer and exposure tracking based on personalized characteristics.

USDA Website Redesign

A website redesign of the US Department of Agriculture’s website, giving the page a visual update, as well as a better organized information architecture.

The Christmas Box

This was a study on the non-profit organization, the Christmas Box where we researched what was important for a non-profit and how to showcase that best on their website through a redesign.
